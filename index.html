Projects

Clock tree design and JESD204B implementation

High-speed data converters require a lot of board space such as traces and vias for the interconnections. These exacerterbated board traces and vias can effect funtional performance of the converters, can be the cause electromagnetic interference signals that can challenge the performance of the rest of the electronics in the system. For these reason it is best to select JESD204B compliant converters. The JESD204B serial interface resolve the above mention issues, however, at the cost of a complicated implementation. In this project I go through the process of designing a high speed analog frond end that is JESD204B compliant.
